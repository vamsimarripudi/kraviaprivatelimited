# Month-End Closing Guide

## 1. Review Bank Accounts

Confirm bank balances and reconcile transactions.

## 2. Review Receivables

Check open receivables, overdue invoices, and reminder status.

## 3. Review Payables

Check vendor payables, payment status, and upcoming due dates.

## 4. Review GST

Confirm GST collected, GST paid, input tax credit, output tax, and filing status.

## 5. Review Journal Entries

Check draft and pending journal entries.

Only post entries once they are reviewed and balanced.

## 6. Review Budgets

Compare actual amounts against planned amounts and document meaningful variances.

## 7. Generate Reports

Generate:

- Trial Balance
- Profit & Loss
- Balance Sheet
- Cash Flow
- GST Summary
- Receivables Aging
- Payables Aging
- Budget Variance
- Bank Reconciliation Summary
