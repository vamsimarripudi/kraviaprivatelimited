# Accounting Workflow Guide

## 1. Create Accounts

Create the chart of accounts before recording journal entries.

Recommended account groups:

- Assets
- Liabilities
- Equity
- Income
- Expenses
- Other Income
- Other Expenses

## 2. Record Journal Entries

Each journal entry must include:

- Voucher number
- Posting date
- Narration
- At least two lines
- Equal total debit and credit

Posted journal entries cannot be edited.

## 3. Manage Banking

Add company bank accounts first, then record transactions against the correct account.

Use reconciliation status to track whether a bank transaction has been matched with company records.

## 4. Track Receivables and Payables

Create invoices and receivables for expected customer collections.

Create payables for vendor bills and upcoming payments.

## 5. Track GST

Record GST by filing period using:

- GST collected
- GST paid
- Input tax credit
- Output tax
- Filing status

## 6. Review Reports

Use Finance ERP reports for trial balance, balance sheet, profit and loss, cash flow, GST summary, aging, budget variance, and reconciliation review.
