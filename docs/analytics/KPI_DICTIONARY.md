# KRAVIA Company OS - KPI Dictionary

## Executive Analytics

- Revenue: Sum of stored financial record revenue.
- Expenses: Sum of stored financial record expenses.
- Profit / Loss: Sum of stored financial record profit/loss values.
- Open tasks: Count of active tasks not marked done or archived.
- Compliance alerts: Count of open compliance items past due date.
- Active products: Count of non-archived, non-paused product records.
- Active employees: Count of employee records in active employment states.
- Active vendors: Count of procurement vendors marked active.
- Active contracts: Count of approved, signed, or active legal contracts.
- Tracked assets: Count of active asset register records.

## Finance Analytics

- Revenue: Sum of stored monthly revenue.
- Expenses: Sum of stored monthly expenses.
- Profit / Loss: Revenue minus expenses from stored records.
- Cash balance: Latest stored cash balance.
- Receivables: Sum of stored receivables.
- Payables: Sum of stored payables.
- Net GST position: Stored GST collected minus GST paid.

## Sales Analytics

- Total leads: Count of stored sales leads.
- Active opportunities: Leads not won, lost, or archived.
- Demo scheduled: Leads in demo scheduled stage.
- Proposals sent: Leads in proposal sent stage.
- Won customers: Leads marked won.
- Lost leads: Leads marked lost.
- Conversion rate: Won leads divided by won plus lost leads.
- Follow-ups due: Leads with next follow-up date due or overdue.

## Product Analytics

- Active products: Products not paused or archived.
- Launch-ready products: Products marked launch ready.
- Live products: Products marked live.
- Paused products: Products marked paused.
- Products with risks: Products with risk notes.
- Average readiness: Average stored launch readiness percentage.

## Compliance Analytics

- Open items: Compliance items not completed, approved, not applicable, or archived.
- Overdue items: Open compliance items past due date.
- Upcoming due: Open compliance items due within 30 days.
- Completed items: Items marked completed.
- Waiting for CA: Items waiting for CA action.

## HR Analytics

- Employees: Stored employee records.
- Active employees: Employees in active operating states.
- Departments: Stored department records.
- Pending leave: Leave requests awaiting review.
- Attendance records today: Attendance entries dated today.
- Payroll summaries: Stored payroll summaries.
- Open exits: Exit records not completed or archived.

## Legal Analytics

- Active contracts: Approved, signed, or active contracts.
- Under review: Contracts under review.
- Pending approvals: Contracts pending approval.
- Pending signatures: Contracts not signed and not marked signature-not-required.
- Upcoming renewals: Contracts renewing within 30 days.
- Expiring agreements: Contracts expiring within 30 days.
- High legal risks: Legal risks marked high or critical.

## Procurement Analytics

- Active vendors: Vendors marked active.
- Unpaid vendor bills: Bills marked unpaid or overdue.
- Overdue payments: Bills overdue by status or due date.
- Vendor bill total: Sum of stored vendor bill totals.
- Active subscriptions: Subscriptions marked active.
- Upcoming renewals: Subscriptions renewing within 30 days.

## Operational Analytics

- Open tasks: Tasks not done or archived.
- Blocked tasks: Tasks marked blocked.
- Completed this month: Tasks completed in the current month.
- Overdue tasks: Open tasks past due date.
- Total assets: Stored asset records.
- Assigned assets: Assets marked assigned.
- Unassigned assets: Assets marked unassigned.
- Expiring licenses: Software licenses renewing within 30 days.
