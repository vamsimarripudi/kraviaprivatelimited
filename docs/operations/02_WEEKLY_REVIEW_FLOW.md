# Weekly Review Flow

## Purpose

Use this flow once a week to review KRAVIA operations, open responsibilities, upcoming deadlines, and company risks.

## Recommended Owner

Founder or Director.

## Preparation

1. Open KRAVIA Company OS.
2. Review notifications.
3. Open Tasks.
4. Open Compliance Center.
5. Open Products Portfolio.
6. Open Contacts & Partners.
7. Open Reports if a formal summary is needed.

## Weekly Review Steps

1. Review overdue tasks.
2. Review blocked tasks.
3. Review high and critical priority tasks.
4. Review compliance items due soon.
5. Review compliance items waiting for CA, director, or external action.
6. Review product risks and next milestones.
7. Review partner follow-ups due.
8. Review recent documents added.
9. Review important announcements.
10. Record new tasks for follow-up work.

## Decisions To Make

During weekly review, decide:

- Which tasks must be completed this week.
- Which compliance items need outside help.
- Which product risks need founder attention.
- Which contacts need follow-up.
- Which documents are missing.

## Closing The Review

1. Assign each open action to a person.
2. Set due dates.
3. Update statuses.
4. Add a board or founder meeting record if decisions were formal.
5. Use Reports if a printable summary is needed.

## Weekly Review Rule

Every active item should have a clear owner, status, and next step.
