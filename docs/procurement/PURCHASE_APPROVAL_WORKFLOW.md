# Purchase Approval Workflow

## Purchase Request Flow
1. Founder or Director creates a purchase request.
2. The request records vendor, purpose, estimated amount, priority, required date, and notes.
3. Approval status starts as pending when approval is needed.
4. Founder or Director reviews and updates the approval status.
5. If approved, a purchase order can be created.

## Purchase Order Flow
1. Create a purchase order only after the purchase is approved.
2. Add PO number, vendor, item or service description, amount, taxes, issue date, and due date.
3. Link documents from Document Vault when available.
4. Archive cancelled or obsolete purchase orders.

## Vendor Bill Flow
1. Add vendor bill after receiving the bill or invoice.
2. Enter bill number, bill date, due date, amount, GST, and payment status.
3. Link purchase order and document references when available.
4. Unpaid bills are connected with Finance ERP payables where applicable.

## Controls
- Viewer role cannot create or update procurement records.
- Archive actions are restricted to Founder.
- Every approval and status change is audited.
