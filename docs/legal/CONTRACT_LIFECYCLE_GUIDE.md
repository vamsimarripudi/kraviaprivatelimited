# Contract Lifecycle Guide

## 1. Draft

Create the contract record with title, type, parties, responsible person, status, approval status, signature status, and document reference.

## 2. Review

Move status to Under Review when founders, directors, lawyers, or advisors are reviewing the terms.

## 3. Approval

Create a legal approval record before signing important contracts.

## 4. Signature

Track signature status as pending, partially signed, signed, declined, or expired.

## 5. Active Management

Maintain effective date, expiry date, renewal date, obligations, responsible person, notes, and related documents.

## 6. Renewal or Closure

Use renewal and expiry dates to prepare renewals or mark contracts expired, terminated, or archived.
