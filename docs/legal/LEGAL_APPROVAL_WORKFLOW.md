# Legal Approval Workflow

## Roles

- Founder: full legal access, including archive actions.
- Director: create, edit, approve, and view legal records.
- Viewer: read-only access.

## Workflow

1. Create the contract or legal record.
2. Add an approval request with approver and notes.
3. Set approval status to Pending Approval.
4. Record approval date and notes when approved.
5. Record rejection reason when rejected.
6. Keep all related documents in Document Vault.

## Controls

Every approval create, update, archive, and status change should leave an audit trail.
