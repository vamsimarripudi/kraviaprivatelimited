# Renewal Tracking Guide

## Renewal Fields

Each contract may track:
- Effective date
- Expiry date
- Renewal date
- Responsible person
- Status
- Signature status

## Review Rhythm

Review upcoming renewals at least monthly. Contracts with renewal or expiry dates within the next 30 days appear in dashboard metrics.

## Notifications

When a renewal date is added or changed, the system creates an internal notification for directors.

## Rule

Do not enter fake renewal dates. If a renewal date is unknown, leave it blank.
